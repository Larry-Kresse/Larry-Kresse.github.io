About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary engineer with deep expertise across electrical, mechanical, software, and industrial design. I work close to the metal, building systems that span the full stack from silicon to enclosure. My passion is solving complex problems that require understanding multiple domains simultaneously.

Whether it's designing custom PCBs, embedded firmware, precision mechanical systems, or the enclosure someone actually holds in their hands, I enjoy the challenge of integrating diverse disciplines into cohesive, elegant products.
